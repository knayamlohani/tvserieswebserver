Welcome to TVSeries Web Server.

To download information about your favourite TV Shows just a make request to https://tvserieswebserver.herokuapp.com/

Searching a TV Show


https://tvserieswebserver.herokuapp.com/series/<seriesName>

Get Series Details By its TVDB ID


https://tvserieswebserver.herokuapp.com/series/seriesId/<seriesTvdbId>/seriesOnly

Get Cast Details


https://tvserieswebserver.herokuapp.com/series/seriesId/<seriesTvdbId>/actors

Get Banners


https://tvserieswebserver.herokuapp.com/series/seriesId/<seriesTvdbId>/banners

Get Complete Series Information with Cast and Banners


https://tvserieswebserver.herokuapp.com/series/seriesId/<seriesTvdbId>/seriesPlusActorsPlusBanners
